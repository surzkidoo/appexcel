Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to the most common questions about our forex trading courses

Do I need any prior trading experience to start?

+

No prior experience is required! Our Forex Fundamentals course is designed specifically for complete beginners. We start with the basics and gradually build up your knowledge and skills. Many of our most successful students started with zero trading experience.

How much money do I need to start trading?

+

You can start learning with our demo accounts that require no real money. For live trading, we recommend starting with at least $500-$1000, though you can begin with as little as $100. We emphasize proper risk management regardless of your account size.

How long does it take to complete a course?

+

Course duration varies by level: Beginner (4-6 weeks), Intermediate (6-8 weeks), and Expert (8-12 weeks). However, you have lifetime access to all materials, so you can learn at your own pace. Most students spend 2-3 hours per week studying.

Do you offer any guarantees?

+

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the course content. However, we cannot guarantee trading profits as forex trading involves risk and individual results vary based on many factors including market conditions and personal discipline.

What kind of support do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive support including: 24/7 email support, live chat during business hours, weekly Q&A sessions with instructors, access to our private community forum, and one-on-one mentorship sessions for advanced course students.

Can I access the courses on mobile devices?

+

Yes! Our learning platform is fully responsive and works on all devices - desktop, tablet, and mobile. You can download course materials for offline viewing and our mobile app allows you to learn on the go.

Do you help with broker selection?

+

We provide guidance on choosing reputable brokers, understanding spreads and fees, and setting up your trading accounts. We have partnerships with several regulated brokers and can help you find the best fit for your needs.

Are there any ongoing costs after the course?

+

No ongoing costs for course access - you get lifetime access to all materials and updates. Optional services like continued mentorship, advanced trading signals, or live trading room access are available for additional fees, but these are completely optional.
